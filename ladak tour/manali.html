Leh to Manali Bike Trip 2021 Overview

Surrounded by the epic Himalayas, Leh is a little paradise on earth. The turquoise-colored Pangong Lake, rugged valleys, iconic Magnetic Hill, age-old monasteries, the magical confluence of two beautiful rivers, etc are the top attractions of Leh that take you away from the chaos and bustle of city life.

Manali is one of the most beautiful hill stations in India with jaw-dropping views, dense forests, snow-capped slopes, sprawling meadows carpeted with flowers. Blessed by mother nature, Leh and Manali are two gems that offer you to take an adventurous bike ride and explore the beauty of the city.

Quick info

  • Route: Leh-Nubra via Khardung La Pass-Pangong Lake-Leh via Chang La-Jispa-Manali-Delhi.
  • Start point: Leh
  • Endpoint: Delhi
