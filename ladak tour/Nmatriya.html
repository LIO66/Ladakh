Maitreya Buddha

About Maitreya Buddha in Nubra Valley, Ladakh

There are many things to fall in love with in Nubra Valley, but one which leaves you fascinated is the 108 feet tall statue of Maitreya Buddha. Everything about it, from its location to its size & aesthetics, makes it a major tourist attraction in Nubra Valley.

The 108 feet tall statue of Maitreya Buddha is located just below the Diskit monastery, at an altitude of 10,308 feet, 15 kilometers northwest off the Khalsar-Panakil route, right at the edge of the desert in the Nubra Valley.

While the Diskit Monastery is a major tourist place to visit in Nubra Valley in itself, you cannot miss out on the Maitreya Buddha statue.

About the Maitreya Buddha Statue

The height of the Maitreya Buddha statue is certainly something to think about, but there are other things about the statue that are as interesting. It represents 3 ideas: protection of the Diskit Village, promotion of world peace & prevention of further war with Pakistan. The gold and red coloured Mitreya Buddha statue faces the Shyok River towards Pakistan and is seated below the Diskit Monastery above a hill.
