Yarab Tso Lake

About Yarab Tso Lake Nubra Valley, Ladakh

Yarab Tso Lake is a famous tourist attraction in Nubra Valley, located almost 15 kilometers from Diskit near to Simur village. Although you have to hike for 20 minutes approx. to reach the lake, the picturesque scenery that awaits you there is completely worth it.

Yarab Tso Lake : What makes it so famous?

Among the many places to visit in Nubra Valley, what makes Yarab Tso Lake special is that it is one of the holiest high-altitude lakes in the valley. The lake consists of crystal-clear water, and perfectly reflects its surroundings. The remote location no doubt adds to its mystique and beauty. You should visit the lake for its serenity and mystical aura.

How to Reach?

Yarab Tso is trekkable from Panamik Village. A 20-minute walk up the mountain will make one reach the lake.
