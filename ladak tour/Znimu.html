Nimu

About Nimu Ladakh

Popular for its spectacular site of the confluence also called Sangam of Indus and Zanskar River, Nimu is a not to miss place near Leh. It is not only the confluence that makes this place a must-see, but Nimu is also the starting point of the All India Rafting Expedition that takes place in the Indus River.

Top Things to see in Samstanling Monastery

Samstanling Monastery was founded by Lama Tsultim Nima almost 140 years ago, and is home to around 50 monks. There are two assemblies inside the monastery, and both of them are adorned with murals, Buddha paintings, 4 heavenly kings & other guardians. But wait, that’s not all, the Magnetic Hill, known for its optical illusion that blows one mind’s away, also sits in close proximity and makes for another sightseeing tour attraction in Nimu. For those looking for an authentic experience of Ladakh, Nimu has a luxury heritage hotel offering a memorable stay near Leh by pampering you in the best way; catering to all your needs; and acquainting you with the culture and traditions of Ladakh.

Major Highlights of Nimu :

  • Indus and Zanskar Rivers Confluence/Sangam
  • Rafting Expeditions
  • Luxury Heritage Hotel
  • Magnetic Hill

Best Time to Visit Nimu :

The best time to visit Nimu is between June and September when the weather in Ladakh is pleasant and the roads are cleared off of the snow.
